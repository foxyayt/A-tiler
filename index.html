Details.

Everything you need to know about our process.

Where are you based?
We are based in Glasgow and cover surrounding areas including the West End, Southside, Bearsden, Milngavie, and Newton Mearns.
What is a bespoke mirror mosaic?
Unlike pre-bought sheets, bespoke mirror mosaics are designed to your wall dimensions. We cut and arrange mirror glass to create a unique, shimmering effect.
Do I supply the tiles?
Most clients purchase their own tiles to get the exact style they want. If you want, we can advise on suitable suppliers and what to look for.
How do I book?
Call to discuss the job. We can quote from details and photos, or arrange a site visit for larger projects.
